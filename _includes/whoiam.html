who i am

I learn, therefore I am

I´m Rubén Salado, a PhD. student in computer science, who is passionate about (but not limited to) data-centric computing: big data, data science, distributed architectures, and more.

My research focuses on how to bring the processing of large amounts of data closer to domain experts, independently of their computation skills. To this end, it is necessary to develop new mechanisms that facilitate the use of data science techniques and technologies to computing non-experts by, e.g., reusing and adapting generic data-intensive techniques to specific application domains, enabling the high level definition of complex data processing applications or managing their execution over the most suitable computing platforms in a transparent way.

Member of the Knowledge Discovery and Intelligent Systems (KDIS) research group at University of Córdoba.
